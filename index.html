The problem

The work that ends in Excel takes most of your close.

Finance teams spend 5+ days every month on the same shape of work — extracting data from NetSuite, applying company-specific accounting policy, building schedules, tying out support, chasing exceptions, and routing for senior review.

The cash flow statement, the consolidation eliminations, the rollforwards, the flux commentary — none of it ships from the ERP, none of it ships from the reporting tool, and all of it is fragile, manual, and the biggest reason your close runs long.

Anatomy of monthly close 17 steps · ~9 days
01 Sub-ledger close in NetSuite ERP
02 Pull GL → 4 reconciliation workbooks Manual
03 Reclassify GL into cash flow buckets Manual
04 Intercompany elimination across 6 entities Manual
05 Tie-out vs. prior period · controller review Manual
06 Final reporting package ERP
Steps 02–05 — the spreadsheet long tail — eat ~70% of your team's close week.
The solution

AI agents that do the preparation. Humans who keep the judgment.

Sigmaflo connects to NetSuite and your subledgers, applies your company's accounting policy and prior-period treatment, and builds the schedules and statements your team would have built — only faster, with full lineage. What you get isn't an automation. It's a review-ready outcome, with every number tied to its source, every classification linked to the rule applied, and every exception flagged for the reviewer who needs to make the call.
